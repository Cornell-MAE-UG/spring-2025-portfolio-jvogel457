Please select one homework assignment from this course that you found most valuable or educational. Write a 1-2 paragraph reflection discussing what you learned from that assignment and how it will be useful to you.


Reflection: 
Homework assignment 5 was the most valuable assignment to me. In this assignment, I went through the motions of calculating the efficiency and effectiveness of a single pin fin and later a pin fin array. This is not only a great review of all the material we have learned thus far in the course, but also an extremely useful skill for an aspiring engineer. Going through these computations gave me intuition for how a heat sink can greatly increase the rate of heat transfer, allowing something like a CPU to operate at a much higher temperature. The second problem on this assignment was an analysis of the transient heat transfer of a current carrying wire. The problem gave me intuition about how submerging a wire in coolant can moderate its temperature, ultimately bringing it to a steady value. I could see this being useful for a power plant, where thousands of amps are carried through wires. This kind of analysis gives engineers peace of mind that the wires will not melt under the extreme heat transfer.
